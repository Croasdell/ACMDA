Special projects

Some jobs benefit from a longer visit or a site check first. These are planned to minimise disruption and keep everything tidy.

  • Garden room & shed fit-outs
  • Home office setup & cable management
  • Insurance repairs & landlord refreshes
  • Move-in / move-out snagging lists

What’s not included

To ensure every visit is safe and fully insured, certain tasks are not offered.

  • Gas servicing or boiler work
  • Full electrical rewires
  • Roofing above first floor
  • Carpet or vinyl floor fitting
  • Large structural alterations

How bookings work

  1. Share your job details through the booking form at handymanplusvan.co.uk.
  2. Ian confirms the visit time, expected duration and price before arrival.
  3. On the day, everything needed arrives in the van so work can begin straight away.
  4. Payment is arranged after completion — receipts available for landlords and businesses.
